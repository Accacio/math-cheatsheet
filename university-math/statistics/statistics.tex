\chapter{Statistics}

For a $D-$dimensional gaussian we have:
\begin{equation}
  \mathcal{N}(\randomvec{y}; \vec{\mu}_{i},\Sigma_{i})=\frac{1}{{{(2\pi)}^{D/2}}{|\Sigma_{i}|^{\frac{1}{2}}}}e^{-\frac{1}{2}\norm{\randomvec{y}-\vec{\mu}_{i}}^{2}_{\Sigma_{i}^{-1}}}
\end{equation}
